# Mission: RFQ-based prediction markets

## Why
Understand RFQ mechanics well enough to decide whether Onside should use them for fixed-odds parlay/combo execution, and to design the boundary between off-chain pricing and on-chain custody and settlement.

## Success looks like
- Explain an RFQ lifecycle and identify every participant
- Distinguish a quote from a trade and from an on-chain settlement
- Decide when Onside needs a liquidity vault, a market maker, or an RFQ network

## Constraints
- Keep examples tied to Onside, Solana, TxODDS, and combo bets
- Prefer short, practical lessons over financial-market theory

## Out of scope
- Building a production market-making model
- Implementing the RFQ protocol before its architecture is selected
